Keystrokes for the Aftermath soundpack.
f9 f10 Volume up and down.
ctrl-f9-ctrl-f10 Roomsounds volume up and down.
ctrl-f11 Mute ambiance.
f11 Sounds toggle.
control-f12 Socials toggle.
control-p Combat panning toggle.
control-t Street sound toggle.
f12 Save sound settings.
control-s Toggle swim panning.
f2 Open options menu.
f5 Volume checker.
